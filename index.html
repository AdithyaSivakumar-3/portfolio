Project Engineer

International engineering competition — university teams design, manufacture, and compete with formula-style electric racing cars. Team size: 40 members.

Role: Head of department for Powertrain and Low Voltage (LV) systems. Responsible for mechanical design, manufacturing, and vehicle integration of LV components. Represented the team at engineering design events at FS Germany and FS East.

Subproject 1 — Mechanical Design (incl. Cooling) for Enclosures

Objective: Design waterproof enclosures and mounting solutions for LV components, plus cooling for the driverless PC.

  • Waterproofing via nitrile O-ring-based enclosure design.
  • Material selection and FEA optimization of critical mountings.
  • Cooling fan selected using analytical calculations (Bernoulli's equation).
  • Tools: Siemens NX, Ansys Mechanical, Prusa Slicer, Foxglove
Outcome
  • Achieved waterproofing of all LV enclosures.
  • 18% weight reduction in PC mounting via topology optimization.
  • 25% temperature drop using forced air cooling.

Subproject 2 — Wiring Harness Design

Objective: Design and fabricate the wiring harness and accessories for the LV system.

  • Selected and standardized connector types and wire gauges.
  • Introduced wiring harness mat design; prepared drawings and manufactured at Wiretronic AB.
  • Tools: Siemens NX (Routing Electrical), Rapid Harness
Outcome
  • Harness mat reduced manufacturing time by 50%.
  • Standardized components enable reuse in future iterations.

Subproject 3 — Vehicle Integration of LV Components

Objective: Packaging, mounting, routing, and power distribution of LV components with secure, accessible, and EMI-safe placement.

  • Packaged components for ease of access with EMI-safe practices.
  • Cross-functional collaboration for clash checks.
  • Methods: CAD packaging, DFMEA, clearance analysis, harness routing, design reviews
Outcome
  • Optimized routing for easier debugging and weight reduction.
  • Reduced assembly/disassembly time.
  • Minimized rework through early clash checks.
